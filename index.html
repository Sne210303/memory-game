🧠 Memory Game

Setup ⚙️

  1. The game board consists of 16 cards arranged in a 4x4 grid.
  2. Each card has a front and back side. The back shows a unique letter (A–H) that forms a pair with one other card.
  3. At the start of the game, all cards are placed face down.

How to Play 📝

  1. Click on any two cards to flip them over and reveal the letters.
  2. If the letters match, the cards stay face up.
  3. If they don’t match, the cards will flip back after a short delay.
  4. Keep flipping pairs until all matches are found!

Rules 📜

  • Only two cards can be flipped at a time.
  • If two flipped cards do not match, they will flip back over after 1 second.
  • The game ends when all pairs have been successfully matched.
